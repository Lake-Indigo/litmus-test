Is this even a good AI problem?

A short diagnostic of AI fit.

In today's rapidly evolving technological landscape, the allure of artificial intelligence can sometimes overshadow the critical question: is AI truly the right solution? Not every problem requires the complexity, cost, and resources that AI implementation demands. A systematic approach to evaluating AI suitability is essential to avoid costly missteps, wasted resources, and solutions that fail to deliver meaningful value. This assessment framework provides a structured methodology to determine whether your enterprise problem genuinely benefits from AI capabilities or if conventional approaches—such as rule-based systems, traditional automation, or process optimization—would be more appropriate, cost-effective, and maintainable. By carefully examining factors like problem complexity, data availability, business impact, and organizational readiness, you can make informed decisions that align technical solutions with actual business needs, ensuring that AI is deployed where it can deliver the greatest strategic advantage.
